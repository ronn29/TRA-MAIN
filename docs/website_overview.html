Tragabay Career Guidance

What this website is and how it works

Tragabay helps graduating students map their strengths to real career options, generate a professional resume, and schedule guidance consultations with support from machine learning–driven recommendations.

What the platform is

Tragabay is a web-based career guidance portal for students. It blends traditional guidance workflows (assessments, counseling, resources) with machine learning to recommend careers, courses, and next steps tailored to each student’s responses.

Students

Find suitable career paths, download resume templates, and book counseling.

Guidance staff

Review assessments, approve feedback, manage consultations, and curate resources.

Program admins

Maintain questions, programs, and resource lists that feed recommendations.

What you can do here

  • Take a career assessment that measures interests, strengths, and program fit.
  • See career path suggestions ranked by a trained ML model (`ml/` models and weights).
  • Explore curated resources and courses that match your assessment results.
  • Generate or download resume templates aligned with your chosen field.
  • Book and manage guidance consultations with real-time availability.
  • Share feedback; approved comments surface on the public homepage.

How it works (student flow)

  1. Create an account via `Register` and sign in.
  2. Take the assessment (questionnaire powered by `student/assessment_test.php`).
  3. Get recommendations: responses are scored and sent to the ML predictor to rank career paths.
  4. Explore options: view suggested programs, related roles, and matching online resources.
  5. Build a resume using the provided templates to match your target field.
  6. Book a consultation to confirm plans with the guidance team and receive next steps.

How it works (under the hood)

Assessment scoring

Question weights and mappings live in `ml/question_weights_map*.{py,json,joblib}`; they transform answers into feature vectors.

Model prediction

Serialized models in `ml/career_model*.pkl` and label encoders turn scores into ranked career recommendations.

Data + storage

PHP + MySQL (see `db/dbconn.php`) store students, assessments, resources, consultations, and feedback.

Admin controls

Admin pages under `admin/` let staff manage questions, programs, resources, users, and feedback approvals.

Pages to know

  • `index.php` – public landing page with features and student feedback.
  • `student/assessment_test.php` – assessment experience that feeds recommendations.
  • `student/` pages – student dashboard, appointments, and resource views.
  • `admin/` pages – question, program, user, consultation, and resource management.
  • `docs/` – explainer guides for models, training, and defenses.

Key ideas to remember

  • The site exists to remove career-choice ambiguity for graduating students.
  • Recommendations combine structured assessments with ML for ranked career fits.
  • Resources and resume templates keep students moving from insight to action.
  • Consultations close the loop with human guidance and verification.
